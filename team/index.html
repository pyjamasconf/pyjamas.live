About us

Pyjamas Conf started in 2019, a group of enthusiastic volunteers in Brazil wanted to make Python conferences accessible to everyone at home. In 2020, without the foreseeing of the COVID-19, we successfully organized a 24 hours conference that you can enjoy at your sofa and making it a globally organized highly diverse conference. This year, we will continue and welcome anyone around the world to contribute.

Do you wanna help us to make Pyjamas Conf a blast? Want to volunteer? Tell us here.
